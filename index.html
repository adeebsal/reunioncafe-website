Reunion Café is founded on the principle of providing a unique and tasteful dining experience, crafted around a Mediterranean origin. We aim to provide great food and a home-style hospitality without the constraints of a traditional restaurant.

Our flavors range from the distinctive Greek gyros to the spiced and aromatic South Asian curry and kebabs. With our menu, there's something for everyone.

Our lunch and dinner menu consists of our deli sandwiches, salads, and platters. Our platters are served with a choice of white or yellow rice, and come with a side salad and a side of pita and hummus.
