Reunion Cafe

  • Menu

Reunion Cafe is founded on the principle of providing a unique and tasteful dining experience, crafted around a Mediterranean origin. We aim to provide great food and a home-style hospitality without the constraints of a traditional restaurant.

Our flavors range from the distinctive Greek gyros to the spiced and aromatic South Asian curry and kebabs. With our menu, there's something for everyone.

For breakfast, start your day off with one of our signature Mediterranean breakfast sandwiches, served on a bagel or toast. Or go for greatness with one of our made-to-order omelettes served with a side of toast and our home fries. We serve coffee and specialty drinks including espresso, cappuccino, and lattes.

Our lunch and dinner menu consists of our deli sandwiches, salads, and platters. Our platters are served with a choice of white or yellow rice, and come with a side salad and a side of pita and hummus.
